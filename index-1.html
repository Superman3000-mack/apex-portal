Sales Scripts

How to Use These Scripts

These scripts are your foundation. Know them well enough that they feel natural — not rehearsed. The goal of every initial call is one thing only: book a demonstration appointment. Do not try to sell on the first call.

🦷 Dental Office Script

Getting Past the Receptionist

"Good morning. My name is [Your Name] — I'm not calling about a dental appointment. I have a brief business proposal for the practice owner regarding a patient acquisition system. Who would be the best person to speak with?"

If They Ask What It's About

"We help dental practices capture the patients they're currently losing every day — after hours calls, missed inquiries, and emergency patients who can't reach anyone. I'd love to get 15 minutes with Dr. [Name] to show them what we've built. It's genuinely unlike anything they've seen."

When You Reach the Decision Maker

"Dr. [Name], thank you for taking my call. I'll be brief. I've been researching dental practices in [City] and I noticed yours doesn't have a 24/7 patient response system. I've built something that's changing how dental practices capture patients — especially after hours and during emergencies. I'd love to show you a 15-minute live demonstration. Would you be available this week?"

Voicemail

"Hi, this is [Your Name] with Apex Marketing Innovation. I've built a 24/7 patient response system for dental practices — including emergency response at 2 in the morning that no other practice in [City] currently offers. I'd love 15 minutes to show you. Please call or text me at [Your Number]. Thank you."

Dental Objection Handlers

We already have a website.
That's great. The question is whether your current website answers patient questions at 2am and captures dental emergencies automatically. Can I show you what ours does differently in 5 minutes?
We already have a receptionist.
This doesn't replace her — it gives her time back. Right now she's answering the same 10 questions all day. Our AI handles those automatically so she can focus on patients right in front of her.
It's too expensive.
One new implant patient is worth $3,000 to $5,000. If our system brings you one implant patient this month it pays for itself three times over.
Let me think about it.
Absolutely. While you think about it your competitors are answering calls you're missing. I'll follow up in 3 days. Can I text you a quick 2-minute demo link in the meantime?

⚖ Law Firm Script

Law firms are high-value targets. A single missed call could be a $10,000 to $50,000 case lost forever. Lead with that pain point.

Getting Past the Receptionist

"Good morning. My name is [Your Name] — I'm not calling about a legal matter. I have a brief business proposal for the managing partner regarding a client acquisition system. Who would be the best person to speak with?"

When You Reach the Decision Maker

"[Name], thank you for your time. How many potential clients do you think called your firm this week after hours and reached voicemail? Every one of those calls is a case that went to a competitor. I've built a 24/7 AI system that captures every inquiry, answers common questions immediately, and notifies your team instantly. Can I show you how it works in 15 minutes?"

The Revenue Angle

"The average law firm loses 3 to 5 potential clients every week simply because nobody answered after hours. At an average case value of $5,000 that's $15,000 to $25,000 in lost revenue every week. Our system costs less than the value of one case per month."

Voicemail

"Hi, this is [Your Name] with Apex Marketing Innovation. We help law firms capture the potential clients they're losing every day to unanswered calls and after-hours inquiries. I'd love 15 minutes to show you what we've built. Please call or text me at [Your Number]. Thank you."

Law Firm Objection Handlers

We have a receptionist who handles calls.
Your receptionist is valuable during business hours. But what happens at 7pm on a Friday when a potential client has an urgent matter? Our system answers instantly and sends your attorney an immediate notification.
Our clients come through referrals.
Referred clients still research your firm online before calling. If they can't get answers at midnight they move to the next firm on the list. Our system makes sure every referred client gets an immediate professional response.
What's the cost?
Our entry package is $1,000 setup and $150 a month. One case you would have otherwise lost covers an entire year. Would you like to see the demonstration first so you can evaluate the value yourself?

Universal Closing Script

After the Demo — All Industries

"[Name] — every day you operate without this system is a day you're potentially losing customers to competitors. We can have your system live within 7 days. There's no long-term contract — month to month, cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Shall we get started today?"

Stop talking after that question. The next person who speaks loses. Let the silence work.

📋 Website Specification Form — Critical

The Website Specification Form must be completed with the client at the time of the demo and signing. This is how Apex builds the client's website. Do not leave the signing without it fully filled out.

  • Submit it together with the signed contract — both are required before Apex begins building

01
Open the demo — Hand them your phone or share your screen. Say: "This is the type of system your business would have — built specifically for your industry."
02
Show the emergency bar — Point to the red bar at the top. Say: "Your competitors have nothing like this. Patients or clients in crisis can reach someone immediately — any time of day or night."
03
Introduce Aria — Tap the chat bubble. Let Aria speak her greeting aloud. Say: "This is Aria — your AI receptionist. She answers every question a customer could ask, around the clock."
04
Let them use Aria — Hand them the phone and let them type real questions. Stay quiet. Let Aria do the selling.
05
Trigger emergency — Type "emergency" in Aria's chat. Say: "At 2am when someone needs help, Aria captures their info and immediately notifies the business."

Leads & Tracking

How Leads Are Assigned

Leads are assigned to you by Apex Marketing Innovation based on your territory. Each lead includes the business name, contact information, website status, and research notes about their current digital presence.

When you receive a lead contact them within 24 hours. Speed matters — the first person to reach a business owner with a compelling offer wins.

Current Priority Industries

  • Dental Offices — High value, high pain point. Most have weak or no digital presence. Emergency response protocol is our most powerful differentiator in this industry.
  • Law Firms — Very high case values mean one missed call costs thousands. After-hours response is critical and most firms have no solution for it.

What to Look for in a Prospect

  • No website or a website that looks outdated and unprofessional
  • No online booking system — customers must call to schedule
  • No chat or AI assistant on their website
  • Fewer than 50 Google reviews or a rating below 4.5 stars
  • No visible social media presence
  • A business that receives emergency or urgent inquiries outside business hours

Submitting a Completed Sale

After closing a sale email the following to apexmarketinginnovation@gmail.com immediately:

  • The signed service agreement — emailed directly from the contract page
  • Client name, business name, phone, and email
  • Service package selected and setup fee amount collected
  • Payment confirmation from Square
  • Any notes about the client's specific needs

Your commission will be confirmed and processed within 48 hours of sale submission.
